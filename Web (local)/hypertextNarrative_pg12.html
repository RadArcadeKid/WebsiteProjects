Beauty of the Skies

The moon brightly illuminates the entire mountain.
Stars twinkle gently, and light up the scene.
I breathe in the fresh winter air. The snow tickles down
Lately, I haven't stopped to appreciate this kind of thing anymore. I’ve been so caught up with life that I don’t take the time to notice the small things that make me happy.

I see the outline of a temple in the distance -

Should I continue towards the temple?

Or stay and admire the trees?
